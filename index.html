WELCOME

The Houston Zooland's mission is to CONNECT the public to wildlife. Giving them the opportunity to LEARN the importance these animals serve to the environment and humans alike. Inspiring them to PROTECT our animal's wild counterparts in their native habitat.

We integrate wildlife health and care, science, and education to develop sustainable conservation solutions. Conservation is at the heart of everything we do.
