ABOUT US

The Houston Zooland is committed to being a leader in the global effort to save animals in the wild. We are home to over 6,000 permanent residents (our animals) for whom we provide the highest standard in animal care. Every time you visit the Zoo, you're helping us save animals in the wild. We support 49 wildlife conservation projects in 27 countries around the world.

Our Mission

Connect communities with animals, inspiring action to save wildlife.

Our Vision

Be a leader in the global movement to save wildlife.

Our Core Values

Passion: We love animals and are committed to saving wildlife.

Inclusion: We welcome everyone and strive to be accessible to all.

Excellence: We provide the highest quality of life for our animals and an exceptional experience for our guests.
